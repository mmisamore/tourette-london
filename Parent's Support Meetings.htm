Self-help Meetings

Support meetings are held on the second Thursday of each month except July and August.

Meetings begin at 7:00 pm and end at 9:00 pm.

Support meetings are primarily attended by parents of children with TS. Other interested adults are welcome to take part in our meetings to learn more about TS and its effects.

Occasionally, a guest speaker will attend and speak on a topic of interest to parents.

***All children must be accompanied by an adult attending the Support Meeting***

Support Meetings are held at:
Madame Vanier Children's Services
871 Trafalgar Street
London, Ont.

Directions to Madame Vanier Children’s Services:
South on Rectory Street
(from Dundas St., King St., York St., or Hamilton Rd.)

Rectory ends at Trafalgar Street
Turn left onto Trafalgar Street
Madame Vanier is 1 ½ blocks, on the right
