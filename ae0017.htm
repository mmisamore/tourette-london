Transient Tic Disorder

Transient Tic Disorder is one where tics have been present for less than 1 year.

Tics may be motor or phonic.

Tics may be simple or complex.

The DSM definition of TS requires tics to be present for more than 1 year before a diagnosis of TS is given.

On this basis, a diagnosis of Transient Tic Disorder often becomes Tourette Syndrome.

Medical doctors have been known to diagnose TS when tics have been present for less than 1 year on the basis of the other evidence.
