Associated Disorders

Some people with Tourette Syndrome have other disorders at the same time.

These are known as associated disorders or co-occurrences and are sometimes referred to as co-morbid conditions.

One study reports that 88% of people with TS have another condition sometime in their lives.

The most common conditions that occur with TS are Obsessive Compulsive Disorder(OCD), and Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder(ADHD).

Conditions that are known to occur with TS include:

Anxiety Disorders,

Depression,

Generalized Anxiety Disorder,

Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD),

Panic Disorder,

Social Anxiety Disorder(social phobia),

Trichotillomania,

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder,

Difficulties with impulse control,

Impulsivity,

Learning disabilities (LD),

Mood Disorders,

Pervasive Developmental Disorders(PDD),

Autism,

Rage and aggression (Anger Control Problems),

Sleep Disorders.
