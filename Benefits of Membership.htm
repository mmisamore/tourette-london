Benefits of Membership


Direct benefits:  Members receive discounts on TSFC materials, discounts on registration fees at our annual conference, our National newsletter 3 times each year and our chapter newsletter 4 times each year. 

Indirect Benefits:  The TSFC provides information about TS to anyone asking at no cost.  This is done through distribution of our Q&A pamphlet, information packages, information on the internet and our lending libraries.  In this way the information that is available to our members is also available to non-members. 

Your membership helps us pay for the updating, publishing and distribution of information to people who are not yet members and to carry out our programs of informing the medical and education community and the general public about TS. 

Help us carry out this work by becoming a member of the TSFC today or renewing your membership if it has lapsed.
